App Features

TAVI Heart Team App is a multifeatured, streamlining toolkit for cardiologists, cardiac surgeons, specialist nurses and the wider heart team.

Risk Score Calculations

Calculate multiple surgical and transcatheter risk scores at once, including euroSCORE, ACC/TVT, and GAVS II.

Valve Size Calculations

Calculate valve sizings for all commercially available devices.

Patient Record Storage

Save the information required for this scores and sizings, and more, to patient records.

Outcomes

Add in post-procedure outcomes in line with recognised international standards (VARC-2) for efficient audit returns.

Export

Export patient records, either in .csv format, or as a PDF.

UI

Clean, consistent UI throughout.

Risk Score Calculations

TAVI Heart Team App allows you to calculate multiple risk scores simultaneously.

  • ACC/TVT
  • EuroSCORE I & II
  • FRANCE-2
  • German Aortic Valve Score II
  • UK TAVI

Valve Sizings

Calculate valve sizings for all commercially available devices.

  • SAPIEN 3
  • Evolut R/PRO
  • ACURATE neo
  • Portico
  • JenaValve

Outcomes

Add in post-procedure outcomes in line with recognised international standards (VARC-2) for efficient audit returns.

Patient Record Storage

Store all of the information required for these, and more, to on-device patient records.

Each record may then be exported as a fully-detailed, or summarised, PDF.

You may also export these as a CSV file - either per-record, or all records in one file.

Your device's record storage can also be exported, either for backup purposes or to share with members of your team. When importing, choose to either merge the data from the new database, or replace your current data entirely.
